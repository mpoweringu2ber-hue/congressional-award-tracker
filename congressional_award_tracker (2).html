Program Guidelines 📖
Official rules from the Congressional Award Program Book.
🏃 Physical Fitness
Improving quality of life through participation in fitness activities.
Silver Certificate30 hrs — no month requirement
Silver Medal100 hrs — 12 months
✅Maximum 8 hours logged per day
✅Activities must be outside your daily routine
✅Gym class during school hours does NOT qualify
✅Goals must be personally challenging and non-competitive
✅Meal planning can only be 25% of total hours
📚 Personal Development
Pursuing a new interest or advancing to another level in an ongoing interest.
Silver Certificate30 hrs — no month requirement
Silver Medal100 hrs — 12 months
✅Maximum 2 goals per record book
✅No activities that earn class credit
✅Mental health activities like meditation are allowed
✅Do NOT overlap activities with Physical Fitness
🤝 Voluntary Public Service
Sharing time and talents for the benefit of others through community volunteer work.
Silver Certificate60 hrs — no month requirement
Silver Medal200 hrs — 12 months
✅Maximum 4 goals per record book
✅Must benefit the greater community — no private businesses
✅Indirect service can only be 25% of total hours
✅No compensation or class credit received
✅Must be non-partisan
🧭 Expedition / Exploration
Immersion in an unfamiliar environment or culture.
Silver Certificate2 days
Silver Medal3 days, 2 nights (consecutive)
✅Must complete 6–8 hours of immersive activities per day
✅You must plan and control the itinerary yourself
✅Medal level trips must be consecutive days
✅Virtual Expedition/Exploration option is available
📋 General Rules
✅At least 1 hour in a month for that month to count
✅Active months do NOT need to be consecutive
✅No double-dipping — activities cannot overlap between areas
✅Hours are cumulative between award levels
✅Record Books take 6–8 weeks to review after submission
✅You need a Validator for each goal (cannot be a family member)
